Christ The Redeemer

Christ The Redeemer is an Art Deco statue of Jesus Christ in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil.
It was created by French-Polish sculptor Paul Landowski and built by Brazilian engineer Heitor da Silva Costa, in collaboration with French engineer Albert Caquot.
Romanian sculptor Gheorghe Leonida sculpted the face. Constructed between 1922 and 1931, the statue is 30 metres (98 ft) high, excluding its 8-metre (26 ft) pedestal.
The arms stretch 28 metres (92 ft) wide.
